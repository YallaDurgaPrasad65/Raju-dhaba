Our Story

More Than a Meal —
A Memory

Raju Dhaba was born in 1998 from a humble roadside kitchen in Punjab, driven by a passion for bold, honest cooking. Our founder Raju ji believed that great food needs no pretense — just pure ingredients, open fire, and love.


Today, we carry that same spirit into every dish we serve. Every recipe is handcrafted, every spice carefully chosen, every plate served with the warmth of a home-cooked meal.
